# Custom Orders — Returns and Cancellation Policy

This document supplements the standard Returns & Refunds Policy and applies
exclusively to custom, made-to-order, and personalized items.

## Cancellation window
A custom order may be cancelled without penalty within 48 hours of placement,
provided production has not yet started. After 48 hours, or once production
begins (whichever is earlier), the order is non-refundable.

To cancel within the window, email the Accounts Team referencing the order number.
A cancellation confirmation will be sent within one business day.

## Production proofs
Before any bulk custom job goes to press, a digital proof is emailed to the
address on the order. You have 3 business days to approve or request changes.
Failure to respond is treated as approval, and production will proceed.

Changes requested after proof approval may not be possible and will incur a
re-setup fee of $85 per affected item type.

## Defective or incorrect items
If a finished custom item arrives defective or does not match the approved proof,
report it within 7 days of delivery with photographic evidence. Confirmed defects
are reprinted at no charge or refunded in full — the customer's choice.

Colour variation of up to 5% from the digital proof is within acceptable
production tolerance and does not qualify as a defect.

## No-return rule
All other custom items — including correctly produced, approved-proof orders the
customer simply no longer needs — are final. There is no return or refund once
production is complete.

## Address for returns (defective only)
Defective items must be returned to the originating vendor. Do not send returns
to the billing address. Contact the Accounts Team for a prepaid return label.
