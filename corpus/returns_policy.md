# Returns & Refunds Policy

This policy governs returns for all standard catalogue items. Custom orders are
covered separately in the final section.

## Standard returns
Items may be returned within **30 days** of the delivery date for a full refund,
provided they are unused and in original packaging. To start a return, include
the original invoice number with the shipment.

## Restocking fees
| Return window            | Refund                         |
|--------------------------|--------------------------------|
| 0-30 days                | Full refund                    |
| 31-60 days               | Refund minus 15% restocking fee|
| 61+ days                 | Not accepted                   |

Opened consumables (toner, ink, paper) follow the same windows but must be at
least 80% unused by weight to qualify.

## Custom & personalized orders
Custom or personalized orders (engraved items, made-to-order furniture) are
**final** and cannot be returned or refunded once production has started. A
design proof is sent before production specifically so errors can be caught early.

## Refund timing
Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business
days after the returned goods are inspected at the warehouse.
