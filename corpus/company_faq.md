# DataFactz Internal FAQ

A living document for common questions. If your question is not answered here,
email the support team and we will add it.

## Access & accounts
**What are the standard office hours?**
Offices operate Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM local time. The London and
Singapore offices follow their own local hours; check the regional calendar.

**How do I reach support?**
Email the support team at support@datafactz-internal.example. Routine requests
are answered within one business day; tag a message URGENT only for outages.

**How do I connect to the VPN?**
Use the GlobalConnect client with your single sign-on (SSO) credentials. If the
client reports an expired certificate, fully quit and restart it once before
contacting support — this clears the cached cert in most cases.

## Tools
**Which password manager is approved?**
Vaultwarden, self-hosted. Personal password managers are not permitted for work
secrets.

**Where do code reviews happen?**
All changes go through a pull request; at least one approval is required before
merge. The build must be green.

## Facilities
**Is there visitor parking?**
Yes, in Lot C. Register the plate at the front desk on arrival.
